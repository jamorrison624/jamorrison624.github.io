What's with the cinderblocks?

You might have noticed the mention of a wall or seen cinderblocks throughout my portfolio. As I was creating my logo, I wanted to portray myself and attach a story behind it. The J is holding its ground as I find myself doing in my life with how I carry myself and with my confidence. When looking at the M, the lines are slanting as if a wall is starting to fall down. When first meeting new people, I am very reserved and I keep to myself, but as I start to open up, I break down that wall and show people who I really am. My design work allows me to stay true to who I am as a person and as a designer. Each design I create is a part of who I am and that is why I used cinderblocks to represent me breaking down my wall and showing you who I am.
